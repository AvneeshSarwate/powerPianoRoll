Note: this is currently only working with laptop trackpads - normal mouse behavior will be weird (to be fixed soon)
Double click on an empty cell to add a note.
You can drag notes around and resize them.
Hold down Ctrl and move your mouse to scroll.
Hold down Alt and move your mouse to zoom.
Click and drag to highlight a selection. You can then drag/resize groups of notes. You can also hold down shift and click to add notes to a group selection.
Hit backspace/delete to delete notes.
Hit Cmd-z/Cmd+Shift+z to undo/redo note editing actions.
